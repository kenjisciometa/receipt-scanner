## Detailed Profiles

### 1. Restolution — [Detail](../details/pos/restolution.md)

- **Company:** Restolution Oy
- **Website:** https://restolution.eu/
- **HQ:** Finland (offices throughout Finland, plus Denmark, Norway, Estonia)
- **Target Market:** Full-service restaurants, fine dining, chains, hotels, entertainment venues
- **Key Features:**
  - Cloud-based POS with real-time reporting
  - RestoSelfi self-service kiosks
  - Online ordering
  - Multilingual UI (Finnish, Swedish, Norwegian, English, Estonian)
  - Central management for multi-location operations
  - Tablet POS for events and pop-ups
- **Pricing:** Quote-based, not publicly listed
- **Strengths:** Nearly 40 years in Nordic hospitality; local support across Finland; comprehensive back-office; "thousands" of customers
- **Weaknesses:** No public pricing; limited online reviews; older brand may lack modern UX
- **Market Position:** One of the largest Finnish-origin restaurant POS providers

### 2. Winpos (via Kassatiimi) — [Detail](../details/pos/winpos.md)

- **Company:** Winpos Oy (part of Winpos Group, HQ Umea, Sweden)
- **Website:** https://winpos.com/en/hospitality/ | https://kassatiimi.fi/
- **HQ:** Vaasa & Helsinki, Finland
- **Target Market:** Restaurants, QSR chains, cafes, arenas
- **Key Features:**
  - Jeemly POS system (over 900 restaurant installs)
  - Mobile POS app
  - Waiter-specific sales tracking
  - Expandable modular system
  - Recommended by Mara ry (Finnish hospitality employers' association)
- **Pricing:** Quote-based
- **Strengths:** Endorsed by Mara ry; major chain clients (Burger King, Espresso House, Friends & Brgrs, Jungle Juice Bar)
- **Weaknesses:** Quote-only pricing; may be too enterprise-focused for small restaurants
- **Notable Clients:** Burger King, Espresso House, Friends & Brgrs, Jungle Juice Bar

### 3. TuloPOS — [Detail](../details/pos/tulopos.md)

- **Company:** RavinteliSofta Oy
- **Website:** https://www.tulopos.fi/
- **HQ:** Finland
- **Target Market:** Restaurants, cafes
- **Key Features:**
  - POS designed by restaurant professionals
  - Easy bill splitting
  - Payment terminal integration
  - Accounting integrations (Procountor, Netvisor)
  - Free phone support Mon-Sun 08:00-23:30
- **Pricing:** Monthly subscription includes device service
- **Strengths:** Built by restaurant industry people; excellent support hours; easy to use
- **Weaknesses:** Smaller company; limited info on KDS/self-ordering

### 4. Solteq Commerce Cloud — [Detail](../details/pos/solteq-commerce-cloud.md)

- **Company:** Solteq Oyj (publicly listed)
- **Website:** https://www.solteq.com/en/commerce-cloud/restaurants
- **HQ:** Finland
- **Target Market:** Restaurant chains, arenas, amusement parks, multi-location enterprises
- **Key Features:**
  - Omnichannel: traditional POS, mobile POS, self-service kiosks, online shop
  - Kitchen Display System
  - Centralized product management for chains
  - SaaS delivery model
- **Pricing:** SaaS model, quote-based (enterprise pricing)
- **Strengths:** Publicly listed company; true omnichannel; strong in chains and large venues
- **Weaknesses:** Likely expensive for small restaurants; enterprise-focused
- **Notable Clients:** VoiVeljet, Tampere Hall, PowerPark, Padel Club Finland

### 5. Confirma — [Detail](../details/pos/confirma.md)

- **Company:** Confirma (over 30 years in business)
- **Website:** https://confirma.fi/en/segment/restaurants-and-cafes/
- **HQ:** Finland
- **Target Market:** Restaurants, cafes, chains, new businesses
- **Key Features:**
  - Mobile/tablet POS for tableside ordering
  - Centralized menu and pricing management
  - Accounting integrations
  - Nationwide partner network for local support
  - Implementation in as little as 1 day
- **Pricing:**
  - Commission-based: from 0.99% per transaction
  - Fixed monthly fee option
  - Hardware: purchase, lease, rental, or commission-based
- **Strengths:** Most widely used multichannel commerce platform in Finland (their claim); flexible pricing; 30+ years experience; fast implementation
- **Weaknesses:** Broad platform (not restaurant-only); commission model expensive at scale

### 6. Fiest — [Detail](../details/pos/fiest.md)

- **Company:** Fiest
- **Website:** https://fiest.io/fi
- **HQ:** Finland
- **Target Market:** Small restaurants, cafes, bars
- **Key Features:**
  - POS, online ordering, table orders, KDS, payments, analytics
  - Works on tablets, phones, computers (no special hardware)
  - Visual floor plans with real-time table tracking
  - Happy hour campaigns, loyalty discounts
  - Setup in less than 10 minutes
- **Pricing:** 0 EUR/month base; pay only for usage
- **Strengths:** Free to start; no hardware requirements; modern all-in-one; fast setup
- **Weaknesses:** Very new/small (25+ restaurants); unproven at scale
- **Note:** Direct competitor with similar positioning to SciPOS

### 7. Zettle by PayPal — [Detail](../details/pos/zettle.md)

- **Company:** PayPal
- **Website:** https://www.zettle.com/fi
- **HQ:** Sweden (PayPal global)
- **Target Market:** Small businesses, pop-ups, small cafes
- **Key Features:**
  - Free POS app
  - Affordable card reader hardware
  - Contactless and chip payments
  - Basic inventory and reporting
- **Pricing:**
  - App: Free
  - Transaction fee: ~1.25%-2.29%
  - Hardware: Card reader from ~29-79 EUR; terminal from ~199 EUR
  - Pro (restaurant features): ~59 EUR/month + 1.25%
- **Strengths:** No lock-in; low barrier to entry; well-known brand
- **Weaknesses:** Limited restaurant features (no KDS, no table management in basic); hardware reliability issues; PayPal ToS changes
- **Reviews:** Mixed on Trustpilot (5,600+ reviews)

### 8. Lightspeed Restaurant — [Detail](../details/restaurant-saas/lightspeed-capital.md)

- **Company:** Lightspeed Commerce Inc.
- **Website:** https://www.lightspeedhq.com/pos/restaurant/
- **HQ:** Montreal, Canada
- **Target Market:** Mid-size to large restaurants, chains
- **Key Features:**
  - Full restaurant POS (iPad-based)
  - Online ordering, loyalty program
  - KDS (add-on), floor plans and table management
- **Pricing:**
  - From ~69 USD/month (single register)
  - KDS and add-ons cost extra
  - Lightspeed Payments: 2.6% + 10 cents per transaction
  - Early termination fees apply
- **Strengths:** Feature-rich; 24/7 support
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive with add-ons; 1-3 year contracts; early termination fees; iPad-only; mandatory Lightspeed Payments; aggressive sales tactics
- **Contract Terms:** 1-3 year lock-in; early termination fee

### 9. Trivec — [Detail](../details/pos/trivec-caspeco.md)

- **Company:** Trivec (acquired by Caspeco; PE-backed by Verdane)
- **Website:** https://trivecgroup.com/
- **HQ:** Sweden
- **Target Market:** Restaurants, bars, nightclubs, hotels
- **Key Features:**
  - POS, HandyPay (handheld), Trivec Display (KDS), Trivec Buddy (self-ordering)
  - Dashboard with real-time reporting
  - Bill splitting
- **Pricing:** Subscription model, quote-based
- **Strengths:** 8,000+ European customers; established since 1993
- **Weaknesses:** No API; limited presence in Finland; ownership changes
- **Finland Relevance:** Limited direct presence

### 10. E-kassa (Note Shot Oy) — [Detail](../details/pos/e-kassa.md)

- **Company:** Note Shot Oy
- **Website:** https://noteshot.fi/ | https://e-kassa.fi/
- **HQ:** Finland
- **Target Market:** Small restaurants, cafes, retail
- **Key Features:**
  - POS on computer, tablet, or phone
  - Recipe/menu management, alcohol reporting
  - Invoicing and e-invoicing
- **Pricing:** From 29.90 EUR/month
- **Strengths:** Very affordable; Finnish-made
- **Weaknesses:** Smaller player; limited advanced features

### 11. Munchi — [Detail](../details/new-ventures/munchi.md)

- **Company:** Munchi Oy
- **Website:** https://www.gomunchi.com/
- **HQ:** Helsinki, Finland
- **Founded:** 2022
- **CEO:** Mario Henningson (Co-Founder)
- **Funding:** EUR 800,000 pre-seed
- **Employees:** 11-50
- **Target Market:** Growing restaurants, fast casual, QSR, cafes, multi-brand groups
- **Key Features:**
  - Cloud-based POS system
  - Munchi Pay (integrated payment processing)
  - Self-ordering kiosks ("Express Checkout")
  - Table ordering (QR scan)
  - Online ordering (own webshop, commission-free direct orders)
  - Loyalty program (points, rewards, automated marketing)
  - Order Manager (aggregates Wolt, Foodora, UberEats, Bolt orders into one device)
  - Menu Manager (sync menus across all channels)
  - AI-powered marketing automation
- **Pricing:** Not publicly disclosed. Monthly subscription model. Direct orders commission-free.
- **Strengths:** Modern all-in-one platform; official Wolt integration partner; helps reduce delivery platform dependency; commission-free direct orders; fast implementation (~1 week); growing quickly (2x quarter-over-quarter)
- **Weaknesses:** Very young company (founded 2022, pre-seed stage); only 100+ restaurants; no G2/Capterra/Trustpilot reviews; no KDS or shift management; pricing not transparent; limited support capacity
- **Traction:** 100+ restaurants since summer 2024; EUR 1M in sales processed in Q1 2025 alone; expanded to Estonia and Abu Dhabi
- **Contract Terms:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Note:** Most direct modern competitor to SciPOS. Similar positioning (all-in-one, modern, affordable).

### 12. Wolt Merchant Platform — [Detail](../details/restaurant-saas/wolt-doordash-capital.md)

- **Company:** Wolt Enterprises Oy (subsidiary of DoorDash since 2022, acquired for ~$8.1B)
- **Website:** https://explore.wolt.com/en/fin/merchant/business/restaurants
- **HQ:** Helsinki, Finland
- **Target Market:** All restaurant types wanting delivery/pickup ordering channel
- **Key Features:**
  - Order management via Merchant Tablet App
  - POS integration with 20+ providers (Restolution, Winpos, Munchi, etc.)
  - Wolt Storefront (white-label online ordering)
  - Self-delivery option
  - Merchant Portal (menu management, analytics)
  - Promotional tools
- **Pricing:** Commission-based: approximately 20-30% of order subtotal. No upfront hardware cost (tablet provided).
- **Strengths:** ~70-80% market share in Finnish food delivery; massive consumer user base; strong brand; DoorDash backing
- **Weaknesses:** High commission (20-30%); previously used exclusivity clauses (ceased Oct 2024 after FCCA intervention); restaurant dependency/power imbalance; not a POS system
- **Contract Terms:** Not publicly disclosed. Previously included exclusivity (now banned by FCCA).
- **Note:** **Wolt is NOT a POS system.** It is a delivery marketplace. Relevant as an integration partner rather than a direct competitor. However, DoorDash's acquisition of SevenRooms (reservations/CRM) signals expansion into on-premise restaurant tech.

### 13. Deliverect — [Detail](../details/new-ventures/deliverect.md)

- **Company:** Deliverect
- **Website:** https://www.deliverect.com/en-fi/
- **HQ:** Belgium
- **Target Market:** Delivery-focused restaurants
- **Key Features:**
  - Middleware connecting POS to delivery platforms (Foodora, Wolt)
  - Menu sync, order consolidation, automated dispatch
- **Strengths:** Solves multi-platform delivery problem; Foodora partnership in Finland
- **Weaknesses:** Not a POS (middleware only); adds cost on top of POS
- **Note:** Not a direct POS competitor but relevant for delivery integration

## Key Differentiators SciPOS Can Exploit

| Competitor Weakness | SciPOS Opportunity |
|---|---|
| Zettle lacks restaurant features (no KDS, no table mgmt) | Full restaurant suite at competitive price |
| Lightspeed has expensive contracts + lock-in | No lock-in / flexible terms |
| Trivec has no API | Open integrations |
| Most Finnish POS have no public pricing | Transparent pricing |
| Legacy systems lack self-ordering | Built-in self-ordering kiosk |
| Enterprise systems too complex for small restaurants | Easy setup, modern UX |
| Fiest is very new (25 restaurants) | More mature / proven |
| Munchi has no KDS or shift management | Full suite including KDS and shift management |
| Wolt takes 20-30% commission | SciPOS direct ordering avoids platform commissions |
