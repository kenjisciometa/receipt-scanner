## Product
- **Core Product:** Confirma POS - multichannel commerce platform for restaurants, retail, and services
- **Key Features:**
  - POS register (fixed and mobile/tablet)
  - Kitchen Display System (KDS) and kitchen printer integration
  - Mobile POS for tableside ordering, terrace, pop-ups
  - Self-checkout solutions
  - Built-in e-commerce (auto-synced with POS)
  - ERP systems
  - Inventory management with supplier integration
  - Accounting integration (automatic sales data transfer)
  - Move POS (combined register + payment terminal in one device)
  - Real-time reporting
  - Multi-location chain management
  - Property management solutions
  - Locking solutions
- **Supported Platforms:** Tablet/mobile POS, fixed terminals, Move POS device
- **Integrations:**
  - **Payment:** Integrated payment terminal (debit 0.39% + 0.03EUR, credit 0.89% + 0.03EUR)
  - **Accounting:** Direct bookkeeping transfer
  - **Suppliers:** Automatic inventory and delivery note updates
  - **E-commerce:** Built-in online store synced with POS

## Pricing
- **Pricing Model:** Multiple options:
  - Monthly subscription (from 88 EUR/month for restaurant POS; promotional first-year pricing available for new businesses)
  - Commission-based (from 0.99%)
  - Purchase outright
  - Finance leasing
  - Monthly rental
- **Payment Processing:** Debit 0.39% + 0.03EUR; Credit 0.89% + 0.03EUR per transaction
- **Setup:** Can be implemented in as little as 1 day
- **Free Tier:** None mentioned, but promotional first-year rates for new businesses
- **Contract Terms:** Flexible (purchase, lease, or rental)

## Market Position
- **Target Market:** SMEs in restaurants, cafes, retail, manufacturing, property management, sports/fitness, wellness. From new businesses to established chains
- **Customer Count:** 14,000+ customers using Confirma products in Finland; 18,000+ subscription customers group-wide
- **Notable Clients:** Not specifically named for restaurant vertical
- **Geographic Focus:** Nordics (Finland primary via legacy DL Software customer base; Sweden via parent)
- **Competitive Advantages:**
  - Largest installed base in Finland (14,000+ customers claimed)
  - 30+ years of POS experience (via DL Software heritage)
  - Extensive local service network (6 Finnish offices)
  - Multiple pricing/acquisition models (buy, lease, rent, commission)
  - Fast implementation (1 day)
  - PE-backed with acquisition war chest
  - Broad product suite (POS + ERP + e-commerce + accounting)
  - Move POS all-in-one device
- **Known Weaknesses:**
  - Brand confusion (recent merger of 4+ companies under one name)
  - PE-owned - potential focus on short-term returns
  - Legacy systems from multiple acquisitions may not be fully unified
  - Not restaurant-specialized (broad SME focus)
  - Website and marketing still showing transition artifacts
  - Pricing at 88 EUR/month is mid-to-high for small operators
