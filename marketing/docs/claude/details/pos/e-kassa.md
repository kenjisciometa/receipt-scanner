## Product
- **Core Product:** E-kassa - browser-based cloud POS system for multiple industries including restaurants
- **Key Features:**
  - Cloud-based POS (runs in web browser, no installation needed)
  - Table sales and bill splitting
  - Kitchen printer integration (direct order transmission)
  - Self-service ordering (touchscreen or barcode)
  - Recipe management
  - Customizable menus with sides, seasonings, special requests
  - Dine-in, terrace, and delivery support
  - Alcohol sales reporting
  - Tip/gratuity handling
  - Invoicing and electronic/web invoices
  - Customer registry
  - Product registry
  - Inventory management
  - Multi-location scalability
  - Self-checkout kiosk option
  - Appointment booking (via Evaraus)
  - Card payment terminal integration (auto amount transfer)
- **Supported Platforms:** Web browser-based - works on PC, tablet, mobile phone. Any device with internet access
- **Integrations:**
  - **Payment:** Card payment terminals (integrated, commission from 0.39%)
  - **Accounting:** Netvisor
  - **E-commerce:** WooCommerce, Shopify
  - **Booking:** Evaraus appointment system
  - **Logistics:** Shipit fulfillment
  - **ERP:** Fennoa (via API)
  - **Other:** Note Shot Finance (invoicing/billing module)

## Pricing
- **Pricing Model:** Monthly subscription + transaction commission
  - E-kassa software: EUR 29.90/month
  - Card payment processing: from 0.39%
  - Self-checkout kiosk: from EUR 48.90/month
- **Hardware:** Hardware packages available (kassapaketit) - POS bundles, receipt printers, computers
- **Free Tier:** 30-day free trial available
- **Free Version:** Note Shot Finance (basic invoicing/POS) available as free download
- **Contract Terms:** Not publicly detailed
