## Product
- **Core Product:** Fiest - all-in-one restaurant management platform with POS, online ordering, KDS, and analytics
- **Key Features:**
  - Point of Sale (POS) system
  - Online ordering with QR code capability (online ordering was built first)
  - Kitchen Display System (KDS) for real-time order visibility
  - Analytics and reporting tools
  - Tab and bill management
  - Menu customization and scheduling
  - Digital menus
  - Order management across channels
  - Payment processing integration
  - Multi-domain platform (dashboard, ordering, receipts, docs)
- **Supported Platforms:** iOS, iPadOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Linux, and web browser
- **Integrations:**
  - **Payment:** Stripe, SumUp, Viva (where available)
  - **Other:** No other integrations publicly listed (early-stage product)

## Pricing
- **Pricing Model:** Transaction-based with zero platform fee
  - Platform fee: EUR 0/month
  - POS payments: 0.99% commission
  - Online orders: 1.99% commission
  - Payment processor fees charged separately
  - VAT 25.5% additional
- **Hardware:** BYOD (bring your own device) - works on any device with a browser or app
- **Free Tier:** Effectively free to start (no monthly fee, pay only per transaction)
- **Contract Terms:** No fixed contracts, no lock-in
