## Product
- **Core Product:** Winpos Hospitality - cloud/on-premise POS system for restaurants, cafes, hotels, arenas, leisure, ferry & cruise
- **Key Features:**
  - Table management with visual floor maps
  - Kitchen Display System (KDS)
  - Inventory management with automatic stock tracking
  - Real-time sales dashboard with graphical visualization
  - Staff/waiter performance analytics
  - Customer management and targeted promotions
  - Mobile POS for tableside ordering
  - Self-service terminals
  - Online ordering integration
  - Multi-site chain management
  - Hotel PMS integration
  - Ticket sales integration
- **Supported Platforms:** Windows-based POS terminals, mobile devices. Proprietary software
- **Integrations:**
  - **Payment:** Nets, WestPay, Verifone, Mash, Klarna, Svea, Ecster
  - **Accounting:** Procountor, Netvisor (daily data transfer)
  - **Other:** Hotel reservation systems, ticketing systems, e-commerce platforms, booking systems

## Pricing
- **Pricing Model:** Not publicly disclosed. Custom quotes based on business size
- **Hardware:** Sold or leased through Kassatiimi and other partners
- **Contract Terms:** Not publicly available

## Market Position
- **Target Market:** Restaurants, cafes, hotels, arenas, leisure centers, ferry/cruise - from single locations to multinational chains
- **Scale:** 500+ customers, 10,000-15,000 POS terminals delivered across 9 countries
- **Notable Clients:** Burger King, Espresso House, Friends & Brgrs, Jungle Juice Bar, Euronics, Sarkanniemi (leisure park), Great Burger, Hotel Haaga, Rolls, Rami's Patisserie, Berny's
- **Competitive Advantages:**
  - Long track record (since 1997)
  - Wide industry coverage (hospitality + retail + arenas + cruise)
  - International presence (9 countries)
  - Comprehensive payment terminal support
  - Strong local service network via Kassatiimi and other partners
- **Known Weaknesses:**
  - Operating at a loss in 2024
  - Legacy Windows-centric architecture
  - Pricing not transparent
  - Not cloud-native (compared to newer competitors)
  - Smaller scale than large international POS providers
