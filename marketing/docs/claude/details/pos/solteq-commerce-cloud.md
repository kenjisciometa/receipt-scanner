# Solteq Commerce Cloud (Solteq Oyj)

## Company Overview
- **Legal Name:** Solteq Oyj (publicly listed)
- **Y-tunnus:** 0490484-0
- **Website:** https://www.solteq.com/en/commerce-cloud/restaurants
- **HQ Location:** Vantaa (formerly Espoo), Finland
- **Founded:** 1982 (as Tampereen Tiedonhallinta Oy); listed on Helsinki Stock Exchange
- **CEO:** Aarne Aktan
- **Employees:** ~343 (2024)
- **Revenue:** EUR 50.9M comparable revenue (2024, full group); EUR 48.8M excluding divested healthcare business
- **Stock:** Listed on Nasdaq Helsinki (SOLTEQ)
- **Operations:** Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Poland, UK

## Product
- **Core Product:** Solteq Commerce Cloud - modular SaaS omnichannel commerce platform for restaurants, retail, arenas, and leisure
- **Key Features:**
  - Cloud-based POS with offline capability
  - Mobile POS for tableside, food trucks, events
  - Self-service kiosk
  - Online shop module
  - Kitchen Display System (KDS) with table and seat management
  - Product and recipe management with variants
  - Inventory tracking (mobile-accessible)
  - Pre-order functionality (off-hours ordering)
  - Gift card creation (omnichannel)
  - Real-time reporting (sales reports, daily reports, cash reports, statistics)
  - Centralized back-end for chain management (products, pricing, reporting)
  - Multi-location management
  - Order flow from all channels direct to kitchen
- **Supported Platforms:** Cloud/web-based. Works on various POS hardware, mobile devices, kiosks
- **Integrations:**
  - **Payment:** Various payment terminals, local Nordic payment methods
  - **Connector Platform:** 70+ ready-made integrations via Solteq Connector
  - **Accounting:** Integrated via connector platform
  - **Online:** Online shop module built-in
  - **Third-party:** Extensive API/connector ecosystem

## Pricing
- **Pricing Model:** SaaS subscription (no upfront server/licensing fees)
- **Scalable:** From single store to full omnichannel deployment
- **Modular:** Pay for modules you need, activate add-ons as needed
- **Specific Prices:** Not publicly disclosed (enterprise custom quotes)
- **Free Tier:** None
- **Contract Terms:** Not publicly available

## Market Position
- **Target Market:** From single cafes to large restaurant chains, event venues, sports facilities, theme parks, arenas. Multi-location hospitality
- **Customer Count:** Not specifically disclosed for restaurant vertical. Solteq serves customers across multiple industries
- **Notable Clients:** VoiVeljet (Turku), Tampere Hall, Padel Club Finland
- **Geographic Focus:** Nordics (Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark) + Poland, UK
- **Competitive Advantages:**
  - Publicly listed company with strong financial backing
  - True omnichannel (POS + mobile + kiosk + online in one platform)
  - 70+ ready-made integrations
  - Offline capability for festivals/pop-ups
  - Proven at scale (arenas, theme parks)
  - Nordic-wide presence
  - Modular architecture allows gradual adoption
  - Strong enterprise/chain capabilities
- **Known Weaknesses:**
  - Large enterprise company - may be over-engineered for small single-location restaurants
  - Pricing likely high for SMBs
  - Not restaurant-only (attention split across retail, utilities, other verticals)
  - Company overall revenue declining (lowered guidance in 2024)
  - Complex implementation for full omnichannel deployment
